In African's Youth the future of Science

Welcome to our organization dedicated to empowering African youth through science education and research opportunities. We believe that the next generation of African scientists will lead groundbreaking discoveries that will benefit not only the continent but the entire world.

Our mission is to provide world-class education, research opportunities, and mentorship to young African scientists, helping them develop the skills and knowledge needed to tackle the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Impact

  • Over 500 young scientists trained across 20 African countries
  • 50+ research projects currently underway
  • Partnerships with leading universities worldwide
  • 100% scholarship support for qualifying students

Who We Are

We are a pan-African organization founded in 2018 with the vision of transforming the scientific landscape across Africa by investing in its most valuable resource: its youth.

Our Story

Founded by a group of African scientists and educators who recognized the immense potential of young African minds, our organization was born from the belief that science education is the key to Africa's sustainable development.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in education and research
  • Innovation: We encourage creative thinking and novel approaches
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of working together
  • Inclusivity: We welcome students from all backgrounds and regions

Our Programs

We offer comprehensive programs designed to nurture young African scientists at every stage of their educational journey.

Youth Science Academy

A 12-month intensive program for high school students showing exceptional promise in science. Students receive advanced coursework, mentorship, and hands-on research experience.

University Partnership Program

We partner with leading African and international universities to provide scholarship opportunities and exchange programs for undergraduate and graduate students.

Research Internship Program

Summer and year-long research internships that place students in cutting-edge research laboratories across Africa and beyond.

Mentorship Network

Our extensive network of scientists, researchers, and industry professionals provides ongoing guidance and support to our students.

Application Requirements

We seek motivated students who demonstrate academic excellence and a passion for scientific discovery.

General Requirements

  • Must be a citizen of an African country
  • Age 16-25 years old
  • Strong academic record in science subjects
  • Demonstrated interest in research or scientific innovation
  • English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS required for non-native speakers)

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts
  • Personal statement (500 words)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Research proposal (for research programs)

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for our programs and become part of the future of African science.

Step 1: Choose Your Program

Review our available programs and select the one that best matches your interests and career goals.

Step 2: Prepare Your Documents

Gather all required documents including transcripts, personal statement, and letters of recommendation.

Step 3: Submit Application

Complete the online application form and upload all supporting documents before the deadline.

Step 4: Interview Process

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview with our admissions committee.

Step 5: Final Decision

Successful applicants will be notified within 4-6 weeks of their interview.
